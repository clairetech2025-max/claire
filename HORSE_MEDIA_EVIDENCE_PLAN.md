# Horse Media Evidence Plan

Do not bulk-ingest horse media into ARE.

The goal is to organize legitimate history and proof without turning private media into noisy memory.

## Proposed Structure

```text
media_manifest/
horse_profiles/
youtube_links.md
seahorse_history.md
mission_assets.md
do_not_publish.md
```

## Public Story Assets

- Approved photos and short clips that show the horse stewardship mission.
- Public-safe descriptions of Seahorse history and Claire Systems mission origin.
- Images that can support a website, demo deck, or NVIDIA/Inception narrative.

## Demo Assets

- Small curated set of approved horse images or clips.
- Each asset should have a filename, date or approximate date, horse identity, public/private flag, and usage note.
- Demo assets should be referenced by manifest, not copied into ARE.

## Provenance Evidence

- Original filenames, timestamps, camera metadata if available, and source device notes.
- YouTube URLs and publication dates.
- Client or rescue history only when approved for disclosure.

## Customer And Client History

- Keep client names, phone numbers, addresses, payment information, and private messages out of public demos.
- Use anonymized summaries unless explicit permission exists.
- Store private evidence outside the repo.

## Rescue And Stewardship Proof

- Horse identity files: name, aliases, approximate dates, care milestones, farrier/vet events when safe to disclose.
- Separate private veterinary records from public narrative material.
- Keep injured-horse or sensitive care details private unless approved.

## YouTube And Channel Links

Create `youtube_links.md` with:

- URL
- title
- date
- horse or event
- public/private suitability
- demo relevance

## Do Not Publish

Create `do_not_publish.md` for:

- private clients
- private locations
- veterinary records
- unapproved faces or minors
- financial records
- legal disputes
- credentials or account screenshots

## NVIDIA/Inception Narrative Support

Use curated, approved media to show that Claire Systems is grounded in real stewardship history and not an abstract chatbot demo.

The horses are central mission assets. Media should support legitimacy, provenance, and continuity without exposing private history.
