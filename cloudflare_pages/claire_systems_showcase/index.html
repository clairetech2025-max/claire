What CLAIRE Is

CLAIRE is a prototype AI control architecture for memory-backed work. The goal is simple: make AI behavior easier to inspect, safer to operate, and more useful in real workflows.

The public demos are intentionally limited. They do not include private memory, secrets, account data, production backend systems, legal files, trader files, or Azure configuration.
