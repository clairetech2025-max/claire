# Claire Enterprise Positioning

## Short Positioning

Claire can sit above enterprise systems such as Salesforce as a governed cognition layer that remembers, verifies, routes, and audits decisions.

Claire does not replace the CRM. It adds governed continuity, policy-aware reasoning, and traceable memory around enterprise workflows.

## Why Enterprises Need This

Enterprise AI risks are often not model problems alone. They are memory, authority, provenance, and control problems.

Common failure modes:

- wrong context
- stale memory
- unauthorized action
- missing audit trail
- retrieval hijack
- policy drift
- unclear decision ownership

## Claire's Enterprise Role

Claire is designed to provide:

- persistent governed recall
- pre-generation orientation
- policy-before-execution controls
- decision traceability
- document and evidence review
- lane-aware routing
- risk-sensitive response shaping

## Salesforce / CRM Framing

Safe wording:

> Salesforce remains the CRM. Claire acts as governed cognitive infrastructure beside it, helping preserve memory, authority, provenance, and auditability across workflows.

Avoid saying Claire is Salesforce, replaces Salesforce, or is a native Salesforce product unless that integration actually exists.

## Use Cases

- compliance review
- financial approval governance
- legal/evidence memory review
- customer support continuity
- partner evaluation workflows
- CRM governance overlay
- document review and traceable summaries
- high-risk workflow escalation

## Buyer-Safe Outcome Language

Claire helps organizations improve decision continuity, evidence discipline, and governed AI behavior. It does not eliminate all hallucinations or replace professional review in regulated domains.
